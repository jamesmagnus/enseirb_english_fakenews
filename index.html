Why this website?

With the recent democratization of social medias, it gets easier and faster to spread information over the internet. These new tools have entirely reshaped our relation with information because most of the people that are using these networks aren’t experienced to deal with informations, and this inability is exacerbated by the overwhelming presence of information on their news feed. We can’t control the way information fluctuates on the internet so we must change the way people behave when confronted to those informations.


The purpose of this website is to enlighten people on the dangers of fake news (their recent impact flabbergasted everybody) and to give them awareness concerning the reliability of news websites. This site keep a classification of news website, a list of rules to help you spot fake news and many other services.

Who are we?

We are a team of young engineering students who feel concerned by the current use of social media to spread information. We all had enough of seeing fake news everywhere when surfing on the Internet, and we decided to try to educate as much people as possible. We wanted to change things. And we also had an english project to do, so.. we combined business and fun. Hope this website will help you.
